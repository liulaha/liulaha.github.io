About me

I am a third year Ph.D student supervised by Prof. Qinghua Zheng at the School of Electronic and Information Engineering, Xi’an Jiaotong University (XJTU). From 05/2015 to 05/2016, I was a Visiting Student working with Prof. Yong Ge at the College of Computing and Informatics, University of North Carolina at Charlotte (UNCC). I received the B.S. degree from the School of Electronic and Information Engineering, Xi’an Jiaotong University, Xi’an, China, in 2013.

  • Public Opinion Analysis
