About me

I am a fifth year Ph.D student supervised by Prof. Qinghua Zheng at the School of Electronic and Information Engineering, Xi’an Jiaotong University (XJTU). Now I am a Visiting Student working with Dr. Lina Yao at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, The University of New South Wales, Australia (UNSW). From 05/2015 to 05/2016, I was a Visiting Student working with Prof. Yong Ge at the College of Computing and Informatics, The University of North Carolina at Charlotte (UNCC). I received the B.E. degree from the School of Electronic and Information Engineering, Xi’an Jiaotong University, Xi’an, China, in 2013.

Research Interests

My research interests include

  • Data Mining and Machine Learning

  • Image and Video Classification

  • Social Media Event Detection

  • Brain Computer Interface
